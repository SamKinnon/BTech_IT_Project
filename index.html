About Us

RP-NGOMA COLLEGE

The Rwanda Polytechnic-Ngoma College is one of the Colleges of Rwanda Polytechnic Higher Learning Institution (RP). Rwanda Polytechnic provides science and technology-based technical and vocational training, enabling beneficiaries to create jobs and contribute to national development.

RP-Ngoma College offers technical and vocational courses leading to a certificate or diploma. The college is located in Ngoma District, Eastern Province, Rwanda.

RP Ngoma Background: Integrated Polytechnic Regional College Ngoma (IPRC Ngoma), formerly known as ETO Kibungo, was established in 1989 by the Government of Rwanda with support from the World Bank.
